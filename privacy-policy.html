Privacy Policy for SPMods Platform

At SPMods, safeguarding visitor personal data streams stands as a core operational objective. This policy outlines precisely how data structures are recorded, processed, or managed during interactions.

Standard System Log Tracking

SPMods follows standard procedures utilizing system log tracking protocols. These files document activity sequences when users access network resources. Information gathered includes IP structures, browser definitions, ISP timestamps, and click counts. This data is strictly aggregated to inspect local navigation trends and map structural layout health.

Google DoubleClick AdSense DART Cookies

Google, as a prominent third-party advertising partner, delivers promotional scripts on our domain via programmatic DART cookie allocations. This tracks network habits to populate relative ad spots based on comprehensive web interactions.

Our Operational Advertising Partners

Advertising systems embedded on our platform may use tracking web beacons to measure ad delivery performance. Our principal programmatic provider remains Google AdSense.

01. Information We Collect
✅ SPMods does not require registration, login, or any personal account. You can browse and download everything anonymously.

When you visit our website, standard web server logs may automatically record basic technical information such as:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website (how you found us)
  • General geographic region (country/city level only)

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily contact us via Telegram or email.

⚙️
02. How We Use Information

The limited technical data we collect is used solely to:

  • Understand which content is most useful to our visitors
  • Diagnose server errors and improve website performance
  • Detect and prevent abuse or spam
  • Analyze download trends to prioritize future updates

We never sell, rent, or trade any data — technical or personal — to any third party. We do not use your data for advertising or profiling.

🍪
03. Cookies & Tracking

SPMods uses minimal cookies strictly necessary for website functionality (such as remembering your dark/light mode preference). We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking, or any third-party cookie networks.

If we use analytics (such as Google Analytics), it is configured with IP anonymization enabled and no advertising features. You can opt out of analytics at any time using a standard browser extension like uBlock Origin.

🔗
04. Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to external services including:

  • Telegram — for our community channel and support
  • Google Fonts — for typography (loads from Google's CDN)
  • APK hosting providers — for file downloads

These third-party services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review. SPMods is not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites or services.

Clicking a Telegram link will take you to Telegram's platform, which is governed by Telegram's own Privacy Policy.

🛡️
05. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect this website and any information it handles. Our website is served over HTTPS with SSL/TLS encryption to protect data in transit.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

🔒 APK Security: Every APK distributed by SPMods is personally virus-scanned using VirusTotal and tested on real devices before release. We never bundle adware, spyware, or any malicious code.
👶
06. Children's Privacy

SPMods is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete that information.

⚖️
07. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data beyond standard server logs, there is typically nothing to access, correct, or delete. However, if you believe we hold any personal data about you (e.g. from a support conversation), you have the right to:

  • Request access to that data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via our contact page.

📬
08. Contact & Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and announce significant changes on our Telegram channel.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us. We're happy to clarify anything.

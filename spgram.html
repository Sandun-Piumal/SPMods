What is SPGram Messenger?

SPGram Messenger is a custom-built Telegram client for Android, developed independently by the SPMods team. Unlike our other apps which are mods of existing software, SPGram is being built from the ground up as our own messaging client that connects to Telegram's network.

The goal is to give users extra speed, deeper privacy controls, and more customization than the official Telegram app offers — all while keeping the core messaging experience familiar and reliable.

The current release, , is an early beta version. Core messaging features work, but the app is still being actively developed, refined, and expanded with new features over time. It is completely free to use — no subscriptions, no ads inside the app.

💡
Good to know: SPGram Messenger connects to Telegram's official network, so your chats sync and behave like a normal Telegram client, just with our extra features layered on top.

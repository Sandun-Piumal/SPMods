How to Install SP WhatsApp

Follow these steps carefully and you'll be set up in under 5 minutes:

    Backup Your Chats
    Open official WhatsApp → Settings → Chats → Chat Backup → tap "Back Up Now". This ensures you don't lose any messages during the switch.
  • 2
    Enable Unknown App Installations
    Android Settings → Apps → Special App Access → Install Unknown Apps → find your browser → toggle ON to allow APK installs.
  • 3
    Download the APK
    Tap the Download button below to get the latest official SP WhatsApp APK (). Only download from our verified link.
  • 4
    Install the APK
    Open your Downloads folder, tap the SP WhatsApp APK file, and tap Install. The process takes about 30–60 seconds.
  • 5
    Restore Your Backup
    When SP WhatsApp opens for the first time, tap Restore to bring back all your messages, media, and contacts automatically.
  • 6
    Login via Link Device (Recommended)
    Scan the QR code from your official WhatsApp → Linked Devices → Link a Device. Much safer than phone number login.

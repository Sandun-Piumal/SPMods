Please read before installing!

    Install the official WhatsApp from the Play Store and register your number there. Download the SP WhatsApp. Install the SP WhatsApp first and open it, then click on the 3 dots and select Link device. With another mobile take a picture of that QR code (you must be quick or the QR may change). Open your official WhatsApp and select Link a device (as if you were going to link with WhatsApp Web) and scan the QR photo you made on the other mobile. Wait for the linking process to finish and and you’re done.
